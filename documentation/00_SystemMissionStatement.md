# System Mission Statement

## Mission

Enable safe, effortless desk height adjustment for office workers to improve health and comfort, with reliable controls that respond instantly and halt motion immediately when released.

---

## Context

- **Target User:** Office worker in commercial building
- **Use Case:** Frequent height adjustments during workday
- **Primary Values:** Safety, reliability, ease of use
- **Key Safety Requirement:** Immediate motion halt when button released or unsafe condition detected
